About Basketball

What is Basketball?

Basketball is a dynamic sport played by two teams, each with five players. The objective is to score points by throwing the ball through the opposing team's hoop. With its fast-paced action, teamwork, and precision, basketball is one of the most popular sports in the world.

Why Play Basketball?

  • Improves Physical Fitness: Builds strength, agility, and endurance.
  • Encourages Teamwork: Develops communication and collaboration skills.
  • Global Popularity: A sport that connects people across cultures.
